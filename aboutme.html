About Me

My name is Dominique, I was born in west oakland,. There is a funny story behind this, I was born at home, its funny because my mom didnt know she was in labor she just thought she had food poisoning because the day before she ate some fast food.

Anyways I am mixed I am Puerto Rican, and Black. My grandparents originated from Ponce and Adjuntas Puerto Rico.

I have a very big family and growing up with a lot of older and younger cousins was kinda hard, because there were only 2 other cousins that were my age so when we would hang out it wasnt just the 3 of us it was everyone and we couldnt keep things to ourselves so we all knew eachothers secrets for the most part.

During holidays or special accation we would go the house of a family member that had the biggest house and we would always have a good time, until someone gets drunk and starts to pick a verbal fight.

To be honest there has never been a year where there hasnt been a verbal altercation, but at this point everyone is used to it.

In my family everyone plays a different sport, but i love to play soccer and volleyball. I enjoy playing other sports but those are my 2 favorite sports. Volleyball season has recently passed which I didnt tryout for, because my mom couldn work it around her schedual. But there will soon be tryouts for the soccer team, which Im excited for but Im also really nervous that I wont make the team. Im a pretty good student, well from what I was from previous years. I currently have a 3.82 GPA.
